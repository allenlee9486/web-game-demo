PokePath TD Route 3-3 Guide: Beating Registeel (Poison Immune)
Route 3-3 is the climax of the third region. The map itself is long and winding, but the real test is the final boss, Registeel.

Players who relied on the "Poison Strategy" for Regirock will hit a wall here. Registeel is a Steel-type, meaning he is 100% Immune to Poison.

With 238,000 Armor and Poison immunity, how do you damage him? The answer lies in the Ghost-type status effect: Curse (Nightmare).

Pokepath TD Route 3-3.webp

🛡️ Boss Analysis: Registeel
Armor: 238,000. (Physical attacks deal ~1 damage).
Immunities: Poison. Do not use Weezing or Arbok.
Weaknesses:
Ground: (Excadrill / Flygon).
Fire: (Charizard / Typhlosion).
Curse: (Gengar / Cofagrigus / Banette).
Pokepath TD Route 3-3 boss REGISTEEL.webp
🏆 The Two-Team Strategy
Because the regular waves require AOE and the Boss requires specific counters, we use a "Swap Strategy".

Team 1: The Wave Clear (Waves 1-99)
Route 3-3 is populated by Rock, Ground, and Steel types.

Core: Grass Types.
Maractus & Sceptile: Placed in the center grass patch. They shred through the heavy Rock/Ground waves.
Sunflora: Essential damage buff.
Excadrill: Placed near the spawn to break armor on elite mobs.
Team 2: The Boss Killers (Wave 100)
When Registeel spawns, PAUSE the game. Sell your Grass units (except Sunflora if you have space) and deploy the Curse Squad.

1. The MVP: Gengar (Level 100)
Role: Infinite Scaling Damage.
Passive: Nightmare. Applies stacks that deal damage over time.
Why it wins: Nightmare damage ignores armor. Unlike physical attacks, it ramps up the longer the fight goes on. Since Registeel is tanky, Gengar has plenty of time to stack this damage to lethal levels.
Placement: Top Right Mountain (or any high ground covering the path).
2. The Clone: Ditto
Role: Second Gengar.
Strategy: Transform Ditto into Gengar. Two Gengars mean double the Nightmare stacks. This cuts the kill time in half.
3. The Stallers: Dewgong & Corsola
Role: Time Buying.
Strategy: Gengar needs time to ramp up damage.
Corsola: Slows Registeel down.
Dewgong: Stuns Registeel in place.
While Registeel is stunned, the Nightmare damage continues to tick.
4. The Armor Breaker: Excadrill
Role: Support DPS.
Strategy: Keep Excadrill. Its Ground-type attacks are super effective against Steel, and its passive deals double damage to armor. While it won't out-damage Gengar, it contributes significant chip damage.
📝 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
Waves 1-99: Play normally with your Grass/Ground team.
Wave 100: Pause.
Sell: Remove Maractus, Sceptile, and other physical units that aren't Ground-type.
Buy: Deploy Gengar and Ditto.
Support: Ensure Dewgong and Corsola are in range of the boss path.
Win: Watch Registeel's health melt away as the purple Nightmare numbers grow larger and larger.
Curse damage is one of the most powerful mechanics in the game. Learn more about status effects in our [Game Mechanics Wiki].
