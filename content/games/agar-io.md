  Entering the world of Agar.io is an experience of simple mechanics and deep strategy. When you start a match in Agar.io, you begin as a tiny cell in a massive, multiplayer arena. Pressing the 'Spacebar' in Agar.io will cause your cell to split into two, launching one half forward to catch smaller, faster players.
