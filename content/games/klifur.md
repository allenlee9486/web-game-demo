### What is Klifur?

Klifur is a revolutionary squishy physics puzzle game that has quickly taken the indie gaming community by storm. Developed by the visionary creator Torfi, this title offers a completely unique take on the climbing genre, moving away from traditional platforming and focusing on a more tactical, physics-driven experience. In this world, players are tasked with navigating a series of increasingly difficult climbing walls, using a "squishy" character that reacts realistically to every pull, push, and swing. This focus on realistic physics is what sets Klifur apart from other browser games.

Unlike many other web-based titles, Klifur prioritizes a meditative yet challenging atmosphere, making it a favorite for those who enjoy both puzzle-solving and physical simulation.
