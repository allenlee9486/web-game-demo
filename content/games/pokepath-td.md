  #### 3. Reading the Battlefield: Your Screen (HUD)

  - **Wave Counter**: Typically located at the top of the screen, this shows the
  current wave number and how many more waves are coming. Keep an eye on it to
  anticipate upcoming challenges and prepare your defenses.

  - **Currency/ PokéDollar Counter**: Usually in the top-right, this tracks your
  available in-game money. Use it to purchase new Pokémon or upgrade existing
  ones. Efficient money management is key to victory!

  - **Pokémon Slots/Inventory**: Often at the bottom or side, this displays your
  available Pokémon and their current levels. Choose wisely and strategically
  place them to maximize your defense.

  - **Health/Lives**: This indicates how many enemies you can let pass before
  you lose the game. Protect this number fiercely!


  #### 4. The Rules of the World: Core Mechanics

  - **Pokémon Placement & Upgrades**: You earn PokéDollars by defeating enemies.
  Use these funds to place new Pokémon towers or upgrade your existing ones.
  Upgraded Pokémon deal more damage, have special abilities, or attack faster.

  - **Evolution System**: As your Pokémon level up through battling, they can
  evolve into stronger forms. Evolving a Pokémon significantly boosts its power,
  making it crucial for tackling tougher waves and bosses.

  - **Strategic Team Building**: Different Pokémon have different attack types,
  ranges, and special abilities. If you get stuck on a particular route, try
  switching up your team composition to counter enemy types more effectively.
  Experimentation is encouraged!
